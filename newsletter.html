Newsletter

For this project, I was tasked to create a newsletter designed towards gamers from teens to young adults, advertising the game design world to any individual who would be interested. I utilized Adobe's InDesign, as the program is applicable towards newsletters and has a wide category of useful features. Adobe's templates proved useful to help me picture what the newsletter could look like, and I use the align tools to make sure that my boxes of text and images had a even look. The final product is what you see above!
